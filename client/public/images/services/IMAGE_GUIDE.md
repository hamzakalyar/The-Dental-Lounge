## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Minimum 800x600px (1920x1080px recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9
- **Quality**: High resolution, sharp focus
- **Color**: Professional, well-lit, clean
- **Style**: Modern, clinical, welcoming

---

## How to Download and Add Images

1. Go to Unsplash.com or Pexels.com
2. Search for the specific service (e.g., "teeth whitening")
3. Download the image (choose large size)
4. Rename to match the filename above (e.g., `teeth-whitening.jpg`)
5. Place in: `client/public/images/services/`
6. Refresh your website to see the images

## Tips for Best Results

✅ **Do:**
- Choose bright, well-lit photos
- Select images with happy patients
- Pick professional-looking clinic settings
- Use high-resolution images

❌ **Avoid:**
- Dark or poorly lit images
- Scary or intimidating dental photos
- Low-resolution or blurry images
- Images with watermarks
